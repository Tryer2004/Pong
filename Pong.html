Hello everyone, this is my ping pong game and I am going to introduce the controls.

To move the rackets you must play with W/S or ↑/↓ arrow keys.

To Pause: press "P"

To switch between multiplayer and AI: press "M"

To change AI difficulty: press 1 (Easy), 2 (Medium), 3 (Hard)

To go the shop press "e"

To gain coins win games

Easy:1 coin, Medium:5 coins, Hard:15 coins
